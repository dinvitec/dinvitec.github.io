Disability invite Creativity NFT

Our mission is to collect and share the arts of people with disabilities to help them express themselves and encourage their creativity.

How it works?

1. Helping

We help to add art of people with disabilities to the digital collections NFT so that collectors can buy it.

2. Training

We train people with disabilities to sell their creativity in the NFT digital collection.

3. Doing

If the author wants to, but cannot create his own digital collection, then we ourselves sell his artworks using NFT, and transfer the funds received to the author.
